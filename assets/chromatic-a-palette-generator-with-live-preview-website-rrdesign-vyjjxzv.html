Accessibility at a Glance

When text sits on a colored background, sufficient contrast is required for readability. The "Aa" on each card shows white text (left) and black text (right). A green dot means it passes — a red dot means it fails. AA (4.5:1) is the legal baseline; AAA (7:1) is ideal.
